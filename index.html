Zimmer in Merhav-am

"Bustan on the Wadi" is zimmer in the settlement Merhav Am. A religious settlement in the Ramat Negev.

The guesthouse is at the edge of the settlement on the edge of a wadi.
Even though the zimmer is inside a settlement and under a house,
it has a sense of privacy and atmosphere and a view of the desert. The zimmer and its surroundings are characterized by authenticity and warmth.

From the zimmer you can see the sunrise from the mountains in the mornings
and during the nights of the middle of the month (Hebrew) the magical sunrise of the moon, and on moonless nights the millions of stars outside..
The settlement has a synagogue, a women's mikvah and a men's mikvah, a grocery store open all day, and a variety of hiking or biking trails that leave right from the zimmer.
